About Us

Oasis Cafe prides itself in the quality and authenticity of its Eritrean and Ethiopian Cuisine. After a complete renovation of our kitchen in early 2014, we are now happy to offer a wide variety of entrees, all of which are completely customized to order.

Coffee is ground and brewed by the cup, and customers are free to blend different roasts to create endless possible combinations of flavor.

Our History

When Taddasse Haile opened Oasis in 2010, he brought with him 20 years of experience running a cafe in Ethiopia. The NoPa district of San Francisco at the time was in need of businesses that could bring the community back together. Oasis Cafe became an integral part in the rejuvination of the Divisadero section of NoPa.
